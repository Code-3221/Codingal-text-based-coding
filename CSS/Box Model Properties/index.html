About pizza

Pizza is a savory dish of italian origin consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes,cheese and often various other ingredients such as anchvies, mushrooms, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. [1] A small pizza is called pizzetta. A person who makes pizza is known as pizzaiolo.
